WHO WE ARE?

We are Focus, a company created especially with the aim of supporting the visually and hearing impaired in their inclusion in society, in the job market, in schools, in sports, etc. In addition, we founded our company to inform and make people aware of the problems that the visually and hearing impaired face in their routine and in specific areas, and always prioritizing the well-being and comfort of these people with disabilities.



At Schools

Within schools, work that should be inclusive ends up playing the opposite role. Accepting the enrollment of deaf and blind students is far from necessary. These students with special needs should be together with the rest of the students, but what we see is the creation of exclusive classrooms for these students with disabilities, who are educated separately from the rest.

In the Business Market

In the labor market, there are few entrepreneurs who open up opportunities for the hearing and visually impaired in their teams. It is up to companies to seek adaptations to the work environment to receive a person who has any type of disability. There are several obstacles faced by these people to enter the labor market, ranging from the requirement for qualified labor, to competitiveness and the false conception of their performance and the preconception that their visual impairment affects their other functions.

On Sports

More than the physical and motor benefits, the practice of activities physical activities, whether focused on competitive sport or just for of health and recreation, is an efficient means of asserting yourself as someone fully capable of having normal life despite the disability. In Brazil there are numerous NGOs and associations that develop projects for the inclusion of people with visual impairment through the practice sporty.
